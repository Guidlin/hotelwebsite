A luxurious hotel where past and present meet

Crowne Plaza is a luxurious 5 star hotel located in the leafy neighbourhood of Govanhill in Glasgow, Scotland. Enjoy the sights of Glasgow from the hotel's rooftop terrace with amazing views of the city. Unwind in the cozy library or in one of the comfortable lounge areas. Whatever your reason for visiting, you're sure to have a memorable stay at the Crowne Plaza.
